Reflection on Readings

"Twelve Pillars" is a self help book by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener. It outlines key principles for personal and professional success. In chapter 4: The Gift of Learning, the authors emphasize the value of continous learning. Seeking mentors, setting aside time for learning and committing oneself to life long learning is a few ithings I picked up on when reading this chapter.

In chapter 5: The Gift of Change, they explain how life is constantly changing and evolving and the needto embrace change and not shame it. Being flexible and setting goals were the main lessons i learnt from this chapter.

In chapter 6: The Gift of Problems, the book discusses how challenges and problems are part of life's journey. It emphasizes that problems are opportunities in disguise, and our ability to solve them contributes to our personal growth.

In chapter 7: The gift of Family, highlights the significance of family relationships and how they contribute to one's success and happiness.
